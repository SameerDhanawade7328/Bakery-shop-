Our Special Desserts


Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun is a popular Indian dessert made from deep-fried dough balls soaked in a sugary, fragrant syrup.


Shrikhand

Shrikhand is a popular Indian dessert made from strained yogurt, typically flavored with cardamom, saffron, and sugar.


Modak

Modak is a traditional Indian dumpling-shaped dessert made from a soft, steamed or fried rice flour dough, typically filled with a mixture of grated coconut, jaggery, and cardamom.


Motichoor Ladoo

Motichoor Ladoo is a popular Indian sweet made from tiny, golden fried gram flour (besan) pearls, which are soaked in a sugary syrup.
